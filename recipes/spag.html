Description

This pasta was Chef BananaYap's favourite bedtime meal. Eating this every night gave him sweet dreams.

"Yeah, this meal really is something else. I always get the best dreams when I eat this. It really is a delight for me to share this with the world. - Chef BananaYap

Ingredients

  • Pasta of choice
  • 1 weiner (needs to be sliced to resemble an octopus)
  • 2 ounces of mysterious fluid
  • 34 oranges

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in mysterious fluid for 40 seconds.
  2. Chop weiner into octopus shape, whilst stacking the 34 orages into a pyramid.
  3. Strain pasta and plate.
  4. Place chopped weiner ontop of pasta, ensuring the weiner's eyes are facing the 34 oranges.

Special comments from the Chef

Enjoy your sweet dreams!
