Fried Rice!

Description

The legendary Din Tai Fung Egg Fried Rice. Simple yet unbeatable. A true classic.

This recipe is made by a world renowned chef Mr BananaYap223, CoFounder of Din Tai Fung Pte Ltd.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 serving of rice
  • 40 tablespoons of msg
  • 1 portion of love

Steps to cook this fried rice :D

  1. Heat your pan of choice (until it glows red)
  2. Add in the msg!
  3. Once the msg has turned brown, add in the rice and eggs!
  4. Lastly, with all your love, stir it and serve it immediately!
